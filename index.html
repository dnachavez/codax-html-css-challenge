Our Story

About Codax

Founded by passionate tech enthusiasts, we're building a community that bridges the gap between learning and innovation.

Our Journey

The Codax community was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to create an environment where tech enthusiasts can learn, collaborate, and grow together without boundaries.

Founded in 2024 by passionate computer science students from the Cotabato region, we recognized the limited tech opportunities in our local area and decided to change that narrative.

Today, Codax thrives as a hub where students and tech enthusiasts collaborate, share knowledge, and elevate one another. Our playful duck logo reflects our fun and approachable nature.
